# ProMed Technologies - CRM & Sales Knowledge Base

## CRM Module Overview

The CRM module manages customer relationships, leads, and sales activities.

## Leads Management

### Lead Sources
- Website inquiries
- Phone calls
- Email campaigns
- Referrals
- Trade shows

### Lead Statuses
1. **New**: Fresh lead, not contacted
2. **Contacted**: Initial contact made
3. **Qualified**: Lead shows genuine interest
4. **Proposal**: Quote/proposal sent
5. **Negotiation**: Discussing terms
6. **Won**: Converted to customer
7. **Lost**: Lead did not convert

### Lead Assignment
- Leads are assigned to sales representatives
- Can be reassigned as needed
- Track all activities per lead

## Customers

### Customer Types
- Direct customers
- Distributors
- Government accounts
- Retail chains

### Customer Information
- Company name and registration
- Contact details (phone, email, fax)
- Physical address
- Delivery address
- Payment terms
- Credit limit
- VAT number

### Customer Codes
Customers are identified by unique codes imported from SYSPRO ERP.

## Sales Activities

### Activity Types
- Phone calls
- Emails
- Meetings
- Site visits
- Follow-ups

### Activity Tracking
- Log all customer interactions
- Set follow-up reminders
- Track outcomes

## Quotes & Proposals

### Quote Process
1. Create quote from customer inquiry
2. Add products and pricing
3. Apply discounts if applicable
4. Send to customer
5. Follow up for response

### Quote Statuses
- Draft
- Sent
- Accepted
- Rejected
- Expired

## Sales Reports

### Available Reports
- Sales by customer
- Sales by product
- Sales by region
- Sales by sales rep
- Monthly sales trends

## Campaigns

### Campaign Types
- Email marketing
- Product launches
- Promotions
- Seasonal campaigns

### Campaign Tracking
- Track responses
- Measure conversion rates
- ROI analysis

## Integration with Logistics

### Order to Delivery Flow
1. Sales creates customer order
2. Order converted to invoice in SYSPRO
3. Invoice imported to Logistics
4. Tripsheet created for delivery
5. Driver delivers and captures POD
6. Delivery confirmed

## Common Questions

Q: How do I add a new lead?
A: CRM > Leads > Add New Lead > Fill details > Save

Q: How do I convert a lead to customer?
A: Open lead > Click "Convert to Customer" > Confirm

Q: How do I see my sales pipeline?
A: CRM > Pipeline View > Filter by status

Q: How do I track campaign performance?
A: CRM > Campaigns > Select campaign > View metrics
