## Phone Extensions

### How to Find Extensions
- Search employee directory
- Ask Welly: "What's the extension for [name]?"
- Check department contact list

### Extension Format
- Internal: 4-digit extension (e.g., 1234)
- External: Full number + extension

## Attendance

### Clock In/Out
- Employees clock in at start of day
- Clock out at end of day
- System tracks presence automatically

### Attendance Statuses
- **Present**: Clocked in and working
- **Absent**: Not clocked in
- **Late**: Clocked in after start time
- **On Leave**: Approved leave day
- **Half Day**: Working partial day

### Viewing Attendance
- Managers can view team attendance
- HR can view all attendance
- Reports available for date ranges

## Leave Management

### Leave Types
- Annual Leave (21 days per year)
- Sick Leave (with medical certificate for 2+ days)
- Family Responsibility Leave
- Study Leave
- Maternity/Paternity Leave
- Unpaid Leave

### Leave Request Process
1. Employee submits leave request
2. Manager reviews and approves/rejects
3. HR processes approved leave
4. Leave balance updated

### Leave Balances
- Check remaining leave days
- View leave history
- Plan leave in advance

## Support Tickets

### Submitting Tickets
1. Go to Support > New Ticket
2. Select category (IT, HR, Facilities, etc.)
3. Describe the issue
4. Set priority
5. Submit

### Ticket Priorities
- **Low**: Non-urgent, general requests
- **Medium**: Important but not critical
- **High**: Affects work, needs quick response
- **Critical**: System down, immediate attention

### Ticket Statuses
- Open: New ticket
- In Progress: Being worked on
- Pending: Waiting for information
- Resolved: Fix implemented
- Closed: Confirmed resolved

## Common Questions

Q: What's John Smith's extension?
A: Search employee directory or ask "What's the extension for John Smith?"

Q: Who's on leave today?
A: Check attendance dashboard or ask "Who's on leave today?"

Q: How do I request leave?
A: HR > Leave Management > New Request > Select dates > Submit

Q: How do I submit a support ticket?
A: Support > New Ticket > Fill details > Submit

Q: Who's my manager?
A: Check your employee profile or org chart

Q: How many leave days do I have?
A: HR > My Leave > View balance
