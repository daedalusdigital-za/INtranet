# Employee Handbook - Leave Policies

## Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to annual leave based on years of service:

- 0-2 years: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years: 20 days per year  
- 6-10 years: 25 days per year
- 10+ years: 30 days per year

### How to Request Annual Leave

1. Log into the Intranet portal
2. Navigate to Attendance section
3. Click "Request Leave"
4. Select "Annual Leave" as the type
5. Choose your start and end dates
6. Add any notes if needed
7. Submit for manager approval

Leave requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for periods of 5+ days.

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.

### Reporting Sick

1. Notify your manager as early as possible
2. Submit a sick leave request in the Intranet
3. Provide a medical certificate if absent for 3+ days
4. HR will update your records

## Parental Leave

### Maternity Leave
- 16 weeks paid leave
- Additional 4 weeks unpaid if needed
- Notify HR at least 8 weeks before due date

### Paternity Leave
- 4 weeks paid leave
- Can be taken within 3 months of birth

- Easter Monday
- Labor Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day (September)
- Thanksgiving
- Christmas Day
- Boxing Day

## Requesting Time Off

For any type of leave:
1. Check your remaining balance in the Intranet
2. Submit request through the Attendance module
3. Wait for manager approval
4. Update your calendar and set out-of-office
