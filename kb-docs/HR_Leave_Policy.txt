# ProMed Technologies - Leave Policies & Attendance Guide

## Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to annual leave as per South African labour law:
- Minimum 15 working days (3 weeks) annual leave per year
- Additional leave may be granted based on seniority and company policy
- Leave accrues monthly (1.25 days per month)

### How to Request Annual Leave
1. Log into the ProMed Intranet
2. Navigate to the People page
3. Go to the Attendance section
4. Click "Request Leave"
5. Select "Annual Leave" as the type
6. Choose your start and end dates
7. Add any notes if needed
8. Submit for manager approval

Leave requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for periods of 5 or more days.

### Leave Approval
- Your direct manager will receive a notification
- Manager reviews and approves or rejects the request
- You will be notified of the decision
- Approved leave is reflected in the attendance system
- Your leave balance is automatically updated

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to sick leave as per the Basic Conditions of Employment Act:
- 30 days sick leave per 3-year cycle
- Approximately 10 days per year

### Reporting Sick
1. Notify your manager as early as possible (phone or message)
2. Submit a sick leave request in the Intranet when able
3. Provide a medical certificate if absent for more than 2 consecutive days
4. HR will update your attendance records

## Parental Leave

### Maternity Leave
- 4 months (consecutive) maternity leave
- Notify HR at least 4 weeks before due date
- UIF claims may apply for salary during leave

### Paternity Leave
- 10 consecutive days paternity leave
- Can be taken from date of birth
- Notify HR in advance

### Adoption Leave
- 10 consecutive days upon adoption
- Apply through HR with supporting documentation

## Family Responsibility Leave
- 3 days per year for:
  - Birth of a child
  - Illness of a child
  - Death of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling, grandparent)
- Proof may be required

## Study Leave
- As agreed with management
- Submit study leave request with supporting documentation
- Typically requires proof of examination

## Public Holidays (South Africa)
ProMed Technologies observes all South African public holidays:
- 1 January: New Year's Day
- 21 March: Human Rights Day
- Good Friday (varies)
- Family Day / Easter Monday (varies)
- 27 April: Freedom Day
- 1 May: Workers' Day
- 16 June: Youth Day
- 9 August: National Women's Day
- 24 September: Heritage Day
- 16 December: Day of Reconciliation
- 25 December: Christmas Day
- 26 December: Day of Goodwill

When a public holiday falls on a Sunday, the following Monday is observed.

## Requesting Time Off (General Process)

For any type of leave:
1. Check your remaining leave balance in the Attendance section
2. Submit a leave request through the Intranet
3. Wait for your manager's approval
4. Once approved, the system updates your attendance record
5. Set your out-of-office message if applicable

## Attendance Tracking

### Daily Attendance
- Clock in at the start of your shift
- Clock out at the end of your day
- The system tracks your attendance automatically
- Late arrivals are flagged based on your assigned shift time

### Attendance Statuses
- **Present**: Clocked in and at work
- **Absent**: Did not clock in
- **Late**: Clocked in after shift start time
- **On Leave**: On approved leave
- **Half Day**: Working partial day

### Viewing Your Attendance
- Go to People page to see today's attendance
- View attendance by date
- Weekly attendance grid shows your Mon-Fri pattern
- Department breakdown shows team attendance rates
