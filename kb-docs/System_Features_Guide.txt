## Welly AI Assistant

### What is Welly?
Welly is the AI assistant built into the intranet. Welly can:
- Answer questions about company data
- Look up employee information
- Check attendance and leave
- Query logistics data (loads, tripsheets, drivers)
- Find stock information
- Search the knowledge base

### How to Use Welly
- Click the chat icon in the corner
- Type your question
- Welly responds with relevant information

### Example Questions for Welly
- "Who is on leave today?"
- "What's the status of load LD-000123?"
- "How many active tripsheets are there?"
- "What stock do we have at KZN warehouse?"
- "Who manages the Cape Town warehouse?"
- "Show me today's attendance"
- "What's John's extension number?"
