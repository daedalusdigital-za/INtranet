# ProMed Technologies - Welly AI Assistant Knowledge Base

## What is Welly?

Welly is the AI assistant built into the ProMed Technologies Intranet. Welly is available on every page as a floating chat bubble in the bottom corner. Welly is powered by a local AI model (Gemma 4 26B MoE) running directly on the ProMed server — no data leaves the company network.

## How to Use Welly

### Starting a Chat
1. Click the chat bubble icon in the bottom-right corner of any page
2. Type your question or request in the text box
3. Press Enter or click Send
4. Welly will respond with information or perform actions

### Conversation Sessions
- Welly remembers context within a conversation session
- You can ask follow-up questions without repeating context
- Clear your session to start fresh if needed
- Each session maintains conversation history

## Welly's Capabilities

### 1. Answering Questions
Welly can answer questions about:
- Company data (employees, departments, attendance)
- Logistics operations (loads, tripsheets, drivers, vehicles)
- Stock and inventory levels
- Company policies and procedures (from knowledge base)
- General information (via web search)

### 2. Employee & HR Actions
- **Look up employees**: "Who is [name]?", "What department is [name] in?"
- **Find extensions**: "What is [name]'s extension number?"
- **Check attendance**: "Who is on leave today?", "Show me today's attendance"
- **Edit employee info**: "Update [name]'s job title to [title]"
- **Reset passwords**: "Reset password for [name]" (admin only)

### 3. Create Support Tickets
Ask Welly: "Create a support ticket for [describe the issue]"
Welly will create a ticket with appropriate title, description, and priority.

### 4. Schedule Meetings
Ask Welly: "Schedule a meeting with [name] on [date] at [time] about [topic]"
Welly will create the meeting and send invitations to attendees.

### 5. Assign Tasks (Todos)
Ask Welly: "Assign a task to [name] to [description] by [date]"
Welly will create the task and notify the assignee.

### 6. Send Emails
Ask Welly: "Send an email to [name/email] about [subject]"
Welly will compose and send the email via the system.

### 7. Track Vehicles
Ask Welly: "Track vehicle [registration]" or "Where is [vehicle]?"
Welly will show the vehicle's current GPS location via CarTrack integration.

### 8. Fleet Status
Ask Welly: "Get fleet status" or "Show me all vehicle positions"
Welly will show an overview of the fleet with current locations and statuses.

### 9. Update Customer Information
Ask Welly: "Update customer [name]'s address to [new address]"
Welly will update the customer record in the system.

### 10. Lookup Addresses
Ask Welly: "Look up address for [location]"
Welly uses Google Maps to geocode and validate addresses.

### 11. System Overview
Ask Welly: "Give me a system overview" or "Show system statistics"
Welly provides a snapshot of current system metrics: active users, loads, tickets, etc.

### 12. Create Announcements
Ask Welly: "Create an announcement about [topic]" (manager/admin only)
Welly will create a company announcement visible to all employees.

### 13. Search the Web
Ask Welly: "Search the web for [topic]"
Welly uses the built-in SearXNG search engine to find current information from the internet and summarize results.

### 14. Backup Database
Ask Welly: "Backup the database" (admin only)
Welly will trigger a database backup.

### 15. TripSheet Workflow
Welly can guide you through the tripsheet creation process:
1. "Show pending invoices" — Lists invoices ready for tripsheet creation
2. "Create tripsheet from invoices [list]" — Creates the tripsheet
3. "Assign driver [name] and vehicle [reg] to tripsheet [number]"
4. "Activate tripsheet [number]" — Starts the trip
5. "Complete tripsheet [number]" — Marks as complete

### 16. Document Analysis
Upload documents for AI analysis:
- PDF files (contracts, tenders, reports)
- Excel spreadsheets (financial data, stock reports)
- CSV files (data imports)
Welly will read and summarize the document content, answer questions about it.

## Knowledge Base Search

Welly has access to a knowledge base of company documents and guides:
- Employee & HR procedures
- Leave policies
- IT support guides
- Logistics operations guides
- Stock management guides
- Tender and compliance information
- Sales and CRM guides

When you ask a question, Welly automatically searches the knowledge base for relevant information and includes it in the response.

## Web Search

When Welly doesn't have the answer in local data or the knowledge base, it can search the internet:
- Uses SearXNG (self-hosted meta-search engine)
- Aggregates results from Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Wikipedia
- Returns summarized results with source links
- No data leaves the company network (search runs through company's own SearXNG instance)

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific**: "What is John Smith's extension?" works better than "extension?"
2. **Use names**: Include employee names, vehicle registrations, or load numbers
3. **One thing at a time**: Ask one question or request per message for best results
4. **Follow-up**: You can ask follow-up questions within the same session
5. **Action words**: Use words like "create", "assign", "schedule", "track" to trigger actions
6. **Provide context**: "Create a high priority ticket for the printer on floor 2 not working"

## Common Questions About Welly

Q: Where do I find Welly?
A: Click the chat bubble icon in the bottom-right corner of any page

Q: Can Welly access my data?
A: Welly accesses company data based on your permissions and can see employees, attendance, logistics, and stock data

Q: Can Welly create things for me?
A: Yes, Welly can create support tickets, meetings, tasks, announcements, and emails on your behalf

Q: Can Welly search the internet?
A: Yes, ask "Search the web for [topic]" and Welly will search using the company's own search engine

Q: Can I upload files to Welly?
A: Yes, you can upload PDF, Excel, and CSV files for Welly to analyze and answer questions about

Q: Is my conversation private?
A: Welly runs on a local AI model on the company server. No conversation data leaves the company network.

Q: What if Welly gives a wrong answer?
A: Welly is an AI assistant and may occasionally make mistakes. Always verify critical information. You can rephrase your question for a better answer.

Q: Can Welly help with logistics?
A: Yes, Welly has full access to logistics data including loads, tripsheets, drivers, vehicles, customers, and can perform logistics actions like creating tripsheets and tracking vehicles.
